Work Process

How we keep painting projects controlled.

From bid invite to final touch-up, the goal is simple: clear scope, clear communication, and no surprise gaps.

01

Bid Intake

We review project name, location, due date, drawings, specs, finish schedule, site conditions, and GC instructions.

02

Scope Review

We identify included areas, excluded work, prep assumptions, product requirements, alternates, RFIs, and schedule risks.

03

Proposal

We prepare a practical painting proposal with pricing, inclusions, exclusions, assumptions, and bid deadline awareness.

04

Startup

Before work starts, we align access, schedule, safety expectations, insurance requirements, materials, and communication flow.

05

Execution

Painting work is performed with attention to prep, protection, finish quality, daily communication, and jobsite cleanliness.

06

Closeout

Punch-list, touch-ups, closeout documents, payment support, and warranty expectations are handled professionally.
